About Me

I am Grace Gundrum, a highly motivated and dedicated individual with a passion for technology and problem-solving. Currently pursuing a major in Computer Science and Engineering at Lehigh University, I have gained a solid foundation in various technical areas such as programming, data structures, web design, and more. My academic achievements, including a GPA of 3.67, reflect my commitment to excellence in learning and growth.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have gained practical experience through my professional roles. As a Tester at iDEAL Semiconductor, I have honed my skills in microchip testing and analysis using advanced equipment and techniques. I also have experience in data visualization and analysis through my work as a Contractor on the Power BI Dashboard Project. These experiences have equipped me with the ability to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios and deliver high-quality results.

Alongside my technical endeavors, I am actively involved in the sports of jiu-jitsu and wrestling. I hold the distinction of being the youngest American female to achieve a jiu-jitsu black belt and have competed professionally both in the United States and internationally. These pursuits have instilled in me discipline, resilience, and a competitive spirit, which translate into my approach to learning and problem-solving in the field of technology.
