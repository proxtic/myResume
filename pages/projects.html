College Performance Dashboard

I developed a Power BI dashboard that showcased essential college data such as grades, GPA, class enrollment dates, and professor ratings. The dashboard consisted of distinct pages dedicated to different aspects of my academic journey. The first page presented my grades and GPA at Lehigh University, providing an overview of my academic performance. The second page focused on NCC grade data, displaying a breakdown of my courses and corresponding grades at Northampton Community College. The third page provided ratings and feedback for Lehigh professors, offering insights into their teaching quality. Lastly, the fourth page featured NCC professor data, providing a similar rating system. By organizing the data into separate pages, the Power BI dashboard offered an organized and intuitive way to explore my academic achievements and evaluate professors.

Test Time Calculator

I developed a test time calculator for my work, specifically for testing semiconductor wafers. The calculator is a web application created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and it is hosted through GitHub Pages. It allows me to input the necessary information to calculate the total time required for testing a wafer. Additionally, I can enter the calculated total wafer time to determine the end time of the testing process. This simple yet effective tool enhances efficiency and helps streamline the testing process in my work environment.

Time Sheet Auto Sender

I implemented an automated solution using Google Sheets and Google Apps Script to streamline my time sheet submission process. Through this system, every Friday night, the hours on the time sheet are automatically updated. The script then generates a formatted email that is sent to the individuals listed in another sheet. The email is personalized for each recipient, addressing them by name, and includes a mention of the week for which the time sheet has been updated. Additionally, the email attaches the updated time sheet for their reference. This automated workflow has significantly simplified and expedited the process of submitting my time sheet, ensuring timely and accurate communication with the payroll employees.
